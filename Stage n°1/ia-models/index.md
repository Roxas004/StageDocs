## Summary

- **OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4.1)** : `+++` Versatile leader in code, chat, reasoning. GPT-4o brings native multimodality.
- **Google (Gemini 2.5)** : `+++` Very good in research, multimodality, and reasoning ("Deep Think").
- **Anthropic (Claude 4)** : `+++` Excellent for writing, reasoning, ethics. Ideal for complex tasks.
- **Meta (Llama 4)** : `++` Good potential, open source, still needs refinement.
- **Mistral** : `++` Very fast in code (Codestral), promising in open source.
